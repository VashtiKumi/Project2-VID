Climate Success Stories

Real-world examples of climate action making a difference

Denmark's Renewable Energy Transition

Denmark now generates over 50% of its electricity from wind and solar power, with plans to reach 100% renewable energy by 2050.

The country has created thousands of green jobs while reducing carbon emissions and energy costs.

Shenzhen's Electric Bus Fleet

Shenzhen, China became the first city in the world to electrify its entire public bus fleet with over 16,000 electric buses.

This has significantly reduced air pollution and carbon emissions in one of China's largest cities.

Costa Rica's Reforestation Success

Costa Rica has doubled its forest cover from 26% to over 52% through conservation efforts and reforestation programs.

The country now generates over 98% of its electricity from renewable sources.

Youth Climate Movement

Young people around the world have mobilized to demand climate action, influencing policy changes and raising awareness.

Their advocacy has helped push climate change to the top of the global agenda.
